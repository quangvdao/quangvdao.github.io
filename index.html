I am a second-year PhD student at CMU, working with Aayush Jain and Riad Wahby. Before that, I got my MA in Math at the University of Michigan, where I worked with Paul Grubbs. I did my undergrad in Math & CS at Columbia University.

Research Interests: My research is in theoretical and applied cryptography, with a focus on zero-knowledge proof systems, post-quantum cryptography, and secure multiparty computation.

Recent Activities: I will be an intern at a16z crypto this summer!
